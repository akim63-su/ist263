Leg Injury Recovery steps
Day Activity Record
1 Rest with Ice Start R.I.C.E method
2 Rest and gentle Elevation Leg elevation for 20 mins every 1 hr
3 Gentle Stretching Continue Stretching when pain is tolerable
4 Return to Icing and light movement on the leg Try to walk with support
5 Strenth Exercise Practice standing with two legs evenly
6 Balance training Increase distance of walking over time
7 Light walking without support Increase the amount of walking gradually
8 Stretch and Strength exercise Increase the sessions and add resistance band into the session
9 Start light jogging Check with doctor
10 Return to Normal Acticity Keep monitoring for swelling or pain
